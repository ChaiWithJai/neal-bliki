CORE PAINPOINTS: VB Specialty Businesses
------------------------------------------
1. "The Invisible Business" (Discovery Gap)
   - Pain: Great services, but no one finds them on Google.
   - Symptom: Low organic traffic, lack of local search visibility.
   - Solution: Local SEO Matrix, Google Business Profile optimization, and Schema markup.

2. "The Booking Friction" (Conversion Gap)
   - Pain: Customers find the site, but booking a service is a chore (phone tag, email back-and-forth).
   - Symptom: High drop-off rate at the point of intent.
   - Solution: Integrated frictionless scheduling via serverless functions.

3. "The Slow-Site Bounce" (Performance Gap)
   - Pain: Outdated sites that load slowly on mobile, causing users to leave.
   - Symptom: High bounce rates, poor Core Web Vitals.
   - Solution: Netlify Jamstack architecture for lightning-fast edge delivery.

4. "The Development Dread" (Time/Cost Gap)
   - Pain: Fear that a new website will take months of their time and thousands of dollars.
   - Symptom: Procrastination on upgrading digital presence.
   - Solution: "VB Specialty Launchpad" - ready in days, not months, starting at $499.
